Core Team

Kevin Farnham

Kevin is an experience design leader who helps organizations envision new possibilities, make sound decisions, and execute skillfully. With a strong focus on systems thinking, Kevin excels at applying a holistic approach to com-plex challenges. His expertise encompasses a variety of disciplines, such as research, stra-tegy, experience design, creative direction, cross-functional team management, and bus-iness management. Throughout his career, Kevin has assisted hundreds of organizations in translating customer and business needs into successful product and brand solutions. He has had the privilege of collaborating with some of the world's most renowned com-panies, including Apple, BBC, Microsoft, MoMA, Nike, Google, and TED Conferences.

Paul Cloutier

Paul Cloutier is a leader in designing businesses. As an entrepreneur–both on the client side and the agency side–Paul has helped some of the world’s most effective brands bring experiences to life. Previously he was Chief Strategy Officer and Principal at the design firm Method. He co-founded and was CEO of 8020 Publishing, a venture-funded media company. He founded Theorem, a ground-breaking Interactive Architecture firm, and spent several years as the managing director of and launching the San Francisco studio of Deepend, the award winning UK based digital agency.

Baykal Askar

Baykal Askar is a seasoned design leader with over 15 years of experience crafting elegant, customer-centric products and services that drive business success. He excels at com-bining user research and strategic thought to deliver meaning-ful designs that foster trust and long-lasting relationships between brands and customers. Baykal has applied his expertise across the full product lifecycle to a wide range of high-profile clients, including Autodesk, Google, Nissan, Samsung, and Steelcase. His work spans defining product strategy, designing UX/UI and motion experiences, and ensuring designs align with both customer needs and business goals to achieve maximum positive impact.
